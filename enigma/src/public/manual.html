1) What Kobecoin AI Guardian Does

Kobecoin AI Guardian is a Solana behavioral risk scanner for trader decision support.

  • Analyzes kill-switch risk and holder concentration before entry
  • Tracks behavior changes during live monitoring
  • Provides structured decision context: favorable, caution, high risk

2) What Kobecoin AI Guardian Does Not Do

  • Does not guarantee profits
  • Does not replace your execution discipline and stop rules
  • Does not remove the need to validate data on external market tools

3) Daily Workflow

  1. Connect wallet
  2. Build watchlist (up to 5 mints)
  3. Run dynamic scan with interval and duration
  4. Review verdict, Market Regime panel (Volatility Index + ADX), and risk details before considering entry
  5. Enable alerts to detect status flips while in position

4) Verdict Interpretation

  • FAVORABLE: cleaner profile, still verify externally
  • CAUTION: mixed signals, reduce size or skip until clearer
  • HIGH_RISK: block by default unless strict override policy exists

5) Pro Mode and Alerting

Use risk presets and thresholds to match your strategy profile.

  • Conservative: tighter kill-switch and connected-wallet limits
  • Balanced: default profile for most users
  • Aggressive: allows higher variance but increases risk exposure

Enable browser notifications and sound buzz for fast reaction.

6) Discovery Mode

Discovery surfaces new candidates, but they are probabilistic and can include high-risk tokens.

  • Always run manual due diligence on every discovered token
  • Do not auto-buy from discovery without strict policy checks

7) Team Operating Policy (Recommended)

  • No entry when verdict is HIGH_RISK
  • Require dual confirmation for CAUTION entries
  • Record post-trade outcomes for process improvement
  • Review missed alerts and false positives weekly

8) Trading Console (Manual + Autopilot)

  • Run Manual Test Flow (Paper) first and review expected drawdown/equity before loops.
  • Use Autopilot Execution Engine only after paper results are stable for your watchlist.
  • Engine guardrails auto-adjust invalid combinations so SL and Trailing Stop stay below TP.
  • Live execution requires PRO plan and backend flag ENIGMA_EXECUTION_ENABLED=1.

9) Holder Behavior Coverage Limits

The holder panel is intentionally transparent about analysis depth.

  • Buy/sell counts sample recent token-account transactions, not full wallet lifetime history.
  • Cluster links use recent signature overlap on top holder accounts.
  • Use Solscan/Birdeye for deeper historical forensic checks before large entries.
