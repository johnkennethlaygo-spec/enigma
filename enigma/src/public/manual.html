How to use the workflows safely and consistently

Dashboard Manual Presentation

Role

Enigma is a trader productivity and risk assistant.

Boundaries

  • No trade execution
  • No manipulation
  • No rule evasion

Workflows

  1. Daily Brief: watchlist context and catalysts.
  2. Risk Check: concentration and suspicious signal review.
  3. Journal: store post-trade notes for discipline.

Viewer Mode

Use the Presentation page for clean summaries in meetings, streams, or team updates.
