Product Positioning

Enigma is a pre-entry and live-monitoring risk intelligence product for Solana token traders.

Scanner VerdictsFAVORABLE / CAUTION / HIGH_RISK

What Traders Get

  • Kill-switch scoring before entry (authority and concentration checks)
  • Holder behavior analysis (connected clusters, new-wallet concentration)
  • Trade plan zones (buy range, support/resistance, stop-loss, invalidation)
  • Live scanner with filter/sort, alerts, and trend/heatmap context

Why It Matters

  • Reduces reaction lag when risk behavior shifts quickly
  • Adds structure and repeatability to trading decisions
  • Creates auditable decision trails for teams and firms
  • Supports extension/API embedding into platforms like Pump.fun workflows

Hard Limits and Policy

  • No trade execution by Enigma
  • No market manipulation support
  • No rule evasion guidance
  • Probabilistic intelligence only, never guaranteed outcomes
